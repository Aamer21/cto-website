Roadmap for FY17

Initiative #1: Agile Delivery
Initiative #2: Digital Services
Initiative #3: Shared Services
Initiative #4: Technology Strategy

We are modernizing GSA IT by first setting up the CTO office with modern design, development, and project management methods. This is happening through foundational projects (e.g., building websites, creating new strategies, organizing around Agile) and projects supporting various GSA IT divisions (e.g., document management, National Staffing Application, LiSaaS). In some cases, it involves working with outside GSA organziations (OSTP/OCSIT Challenge Toolkit, TTS stand-up, open-source with D2D).

This roadmap sets our priorities for the year and provides information for our stakeholders. This roadmap is a rough estimate of what we're focused on completing in the near, medium, and long-term.

The items in the roadmap are subject to change; we'll do our best to keep the roadmap updated as things change.


Initiative #1: Agile Delivery

As an Agency, the GSA transforms to a shared understanding of Agile delivery whose quality can be measured.

CTO Vision element:

Make small bets, Modernize and innovate

Near-term: Oct-Jan
Establish Agile documentation for CTO site - materials including process guides and contract language
Medium-term: Feb-May
Complete project intake process and get projects in the pipeline
Agile investment process defined and socialized through GSA
Long-term: June-Sept
Establish an Agile practice for skills augmentation by coaching
