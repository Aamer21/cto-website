Tech Talks

The GSA CTO team provides by-weekly Tech Talks. This is where members of the team discuss topics found in the guides in more detail. They are 30-60 minute informal meeting-type settings for internal GSA members. Demonstrations are often provided along with follow-up Q&A.

A future Tech Talk schedule is forthcoming and past events will be posted here. Enjoy the PDF files, audio, and video files. You can reach out to the presenter via contact information in the slides or through this site's feedback mechanisms.



GSA Digital Services - New

3/14/17

Technology in the federal sector is rapidly changing with focus on new technologies to meet emerging customer and end user needs. Design elicitation, rapid and continuous software deployments, open source technologies, sprint management cycles, etc., are the new norm. This requires a new digital approach to IT in GSA.

In 2016, GSA IT established the Digital Services Division within the Office of the CTO focusing on modern design, development, and product/project management.

Joe Castle leads the office and will provide a Tech Talk explaining how GSA Digital Service started, its focus, and current projects. Furthermore, he will explain how to engage the division for assistance with your current and future projects.

Open Source at GSA

2/28/17

The Digital Services Division in the Office of the CTO is responsible for assisting with innovation and modernization of GSA IT by promoting policies establish by the White House Office of Management and Budget (OMB) including open government, open data, and open source software (OSS).

In this Tech Talk, Joseph Castle and Jeffrey Fredrickson from the Digital Services Division provided an overview of the OMB and GSA policies, discussed implementation and measurement, and discussed the importance of OSS to the agency.
